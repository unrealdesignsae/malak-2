## 🏆 What Works Best for Your Competitors (That You Should Steal)

### 1. The "Numbers Post" — Best performing format in GCC B2B
Zenith and Starcom posts with hard numbers get 3–5× more engagement than brand narrative posts. Entourage has the source material (60K visitors at Ithra, 12K Iftar meals, G20 scope). **The data exists. Use it systematically.**

```
Example: Your Ithra post → "60,000 visitors. 28 activations. 5 zones. 3 days.
This is what an Eid experience looks like when storytelling is engineered with purpose."
→ Include a metric in the first line of EVERY case study post.
```

### 2. The "CEO Media Moment" Amplification Play
When Mohammed Tayem appeared on Dubai Eye 103.8 — that post got **20 reactions and reach from Dubai Eye's 41K followers**. Competitors like Zenith use CEO quote cards weekly. Entourage has a credible, experienced CEO voice. **Build a regular CEO content series.**

### 3. Story Highlights as a Silent Sales Tool
Every top agency on Instagram uses Story Highlights as a permanent portfolio: "Events," "Campaigns," "Awards," "Clients," "BTS." These are the first things a potential client checks. **You have 989 posts of amazing work and zero of it is organized for a visitor's first 30 seconds.**

### 4. The "GCC Report" Lead Magnet (Hashtag Agency's playbook)
Hashtag Agency published a "GCC Brand Challenges Report" → got covered by Campaign ME → their follower count surged. **You have 17 years of data on mega-events and government communications in GCC. That's a report no other agency can write.**

### 5. TikTok for Event Behind-the-Scenes
Your competitors use TikTok BTS from mega-events to reach Gen Z decision-makers and future clients. The logistics of a G20, the staging of Roshn Saudi League closing ceremony, the build-up to Ithra — this is content gold that has ZERO production cost since it's filmed during live delivery. **One TikTok account activation could generate enormous reach from existing work.**
