## 🧠 Key Takeaways for Your Strategy

> [!TIP]
> **The single biggest opportunity missed by brands:** Most focus on Instagram/TikTok but ignore **Snapchat** — which has the highest daily active usage per capita in Saudi Arabia and Kuwait. Agencies like Hashtag that crack Snapchat get outsized reach at a fraction of the competition.

> [!NOTE]
> **Ramadan is the Super Bowl of GCC social media.** Reach during Ramadan 2024 was 3–4× normal months. All top agencies begin planning Ramadan content in January. If you're not 90 days ahead, you're already behind.

> [!WARNING]
> **What NOT to do:** Post Western content translated to Arabic. GCC audiences immediately identify and disengage from content that wasn't created FOR them. Local cultural nuance isn't optional — it's the product.
