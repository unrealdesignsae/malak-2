## 🤖 AGENTIC WORKFLOW SYSTEM

This is how you use AI automation to multiply your reach without multiplying your team.

---

### WORKFLOW 1: THE "ONE SHOOT → 12 ASSETS" ENGINE

```
INPUT: 1 CEO video recording (2–3 min) OR 1 event BTS clip

AUTOMATED PIPELINE:
  Step 1 → AI transcription (Whisper/AssemblyAI)
  Step 2 → AI clip selector (finds 3 best 30-sec moments)
  Step 3 → Auto-subtitle generation (EN + AR)
  Step 4 → Auto-format: 9:16 for TK/IG/SC, 16:9 for YT/LI
  Step 5 → AI caption writer: 5 platform-specific captions from transcript
  Step 6 → Thumbnail auto-generator
  Step 7 → Scheduler posts across all platforms

OUTPUT: 1 recording → 12 platform-native content pieces
HUMAN TIME REQUIRED: 30 min review + approve
```

**Tools to use:**
- **Opus Clip** or **Descript** → auto-clip selection + subtitles
- **Later** or **Buffer** → cross-platform scheduling
- **ElevenLabs** → Arabic voiceover generation for KSA versions
- **Canva AI** → auto-resize visuals per platform spec

---

### WORKFLOW 2: THE "EVENT LIVE COVERAGE" MACHINE

```
TRIGGER: Any Entourage-produced event going live

AUTOMATED PIPELINE:
  Step 1 → Designate 1 team member as "content capture" role
  Step 2 → Phone footage uploaded to shared folder in real-time
  Step 3 → AI agent (Make.com) detects new files → triggers processing
  Step 4 → Auto-trim clips to 30 sec, add logo watermark
  Step 5 → Draft caption from event briefing doc (pre-fed to GPT)
  Step 6 → Post to TikTok + IG Stories within 2 hours of capture
  Step 7 → Best clips queued for next-day feed posts with polished captions

OUTPUT: Real-time social coverage with 2-hour turnaround
TOOLS: Make.com + CapCut API + Buffer API
```

---

### WORKFLOW 3: THE "COMPETITOR PULSE" MONITOR

```
TRIGGER: Daily automated scan

AUTOMATED PIPELINE:
  Step 1 → Apify scraper monitors: Wavemaker, Starcom, Hashtag, Zenith LI/IG
  Step 2 → AI summarizes top-performing posts from past 24 hours
  Step 3 → Flags any trending topic in GCC marketing sphere
  Step 4 → Generates "reactive post brief" for content team
  Step 5 → If a competitor post goes viral (>100 reactions) → instant alert to comms lead

OUTPUT: Daily 9am "Social Intelligence Briefing" email to content team
TOOLS: Apify + GPT-4o + Make.com + Slack/email webhook
```
